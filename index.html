Founded on October 6th 2021 and operating from June 2024, Globe Media is a Ghanaian-based digital billboard advertising agency with the aim of displaying local and cross-cultural content.

We first came to this idea and concept from the thought of imagining, "What if you could display a public television, free of charge, for all viewers to see?" We then grew further into the idea of specificating on educational videos, documentaries, tv shows, music videos, paintings, photography, sculpture, architecture, art and many more others that truly have the ability to influence people in their ways of thinking and expand their consciousness beyond just what is around them and what they are exposed to on a daily day to day purpose. The foot traffic also doubles as a great customer attraction to the businesses in the area of each billboard, which most likely should be ours.
